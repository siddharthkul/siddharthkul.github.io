Abodemart

Seeing the lack of any platform for students to buy and sell within my University I decided to do build a website and mobile application for the same and was astonished and overwhelmed to see the instant response from so many students. Students could now buy and sell used books, electronics, vehicles and more with others from the campus.

My journey working on Abodemart has introduced me to the different aspects of a business such as marketing and social publicity which I otherwise would not have had a chance to learn. The sense of satisfaction derived from my efforts having made a very small-scale, yet, direct impact has reaffirmed my purpose to help make a difference with innovation.

I have also come to realize that creating intuitive and well-designed products that help solve real life problems will always be welcomed and used by people.
